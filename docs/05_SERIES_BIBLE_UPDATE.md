## 2. The Canon Update — Career Track Activation

In existing /signal canon, SYD handles only Life Directives. The MC improves his habits, builds stats, fights the Stagnation Faction's influence on his own execution. This was the first form of SYD.

The canon update adds a specific moment in the story — around T-015 to T-020 — where SYD's Neural Link recovery process surfaces new data. The System recognises its own prior scope was insufficient. Career Directives activate.

This is not a retcon. It is a reveal about SYD itself. The System is still being rebuilt. It does not have complete data. When it recovers new data that changes its operational assessment, it adapts. The MC experiences this as a new category of directive appearing with no prior announcement.

**The transmission that changes things (T-015):**

```
[ NEURAL LINK RECOVERY: 47% ]
[ NEW DATA RETRIEVED ]

PRIOR SCOPE ASSESSMENT: INCOMPLETE.

LIFE TRACK EXECUTION WITHOUT ECONOMIC CONTEXT IS INSUFFICIENT
COUNTER TO CASCADE.

AN OPERATOR WHO EXECUTES DAILY AND REMAINS ECONOMICALLY STAGNANT
IS STILL LOSING GROUND.

CAREER TRACK: NOW ACTIVE.
NEW DIRECTIVE CATEGORY: CAREER.
NEW OBJECTIVE: RANK PROGRESSION.

[ FIRST CAREER DIRECTIVE: LOADING ]
```

The MC reads this. He sits with it. The System is telling him that what he has been doing — the directives, the streaks, the rebuilt stats — was not enough. Not wrong. Not wasted. But incomplete.

This is the scene. Not drama. Recognition.

## 3. The MC's Career Arc

The MC starts unemployed at T-001. This was always true — his economic situation is part of the established stuck-ness of the opening chapters.

### Career Arc Beat Map

**T-001 to T-014 (existing):**
Life Track only. The MC's unemployment is present but not addressed by the System — it has not recovered that data yet. Small beats of economic pressure are added in the revision (see Section 6).

**T-015 to T-020 (new chapters — the Career Activation arc):**
Career Track activates. The System identifies the MC's career class (Warlord — his background is in coordination and management, unrecognised). First Career Directives arrive. He prepares for a job application through directive chains. Faces the interview Encounter — and fails the first time. The System delivers a precise, cold debrief on exactly what happened and why. He attempts again. Gets hired. First day employed is the arc close — not triumphant, just present.

**T-021 to T-040 (Arc 2 — Incursion, updated):**
MC is now employed — Coordinator rank, Warlord class. The workplace is the world that opened. Career Encounters happen there. Colleague NPCs appear with their own dynamics. The Sync-Link partner (introduced T-012, appears fully T-013) has her own career running parallel — she is an Architect class, which creates natural complementarity and occasional friction. The world of Arc 2 is now larger than it was — two fronts, two types of directives, the compounding of both tracks becoming visible.

**T-041 to T-060 (Arc 3 — Corruption, updated):**
The Narrative Entity's attack now has a career dimension. The seeded doubt extends to the career track — is the rank progression real? Is the System correctly reading his professional situation? Is any of it worth it? The Corrupted State in this arc affects both tracks. Recovery requires recommitting to both. The MC does not get to separate them. They are one system and he has to engage with all of it to exit.

**T-061 to T-100 (Arcs 4 and 5):**
MC rising through Warlord ranks. Life Track stats established. The compounding between both tracks becomes the story's central operating fact — his Intelligence on the Life Track makes him sharper in Career Encounters than his rank would predict. His Charisma in the world opens NPC relationships that give him career leverage. By Arc 5, the two tracks are fully integrated. He is not managing two systems. He has become the system.

## 4. Updated Arc Structures

### Arc 1 Extended Close (T-015 to T-020)

Original Arc 1 closed at T-010 with the MC recommitting after Corrupted State. The extended arc closes on his first day at work.

The tone of T-020 is not triumph. It is the specific feeling of a starting coordinate that has shifted. He was unemployed. Now he is not. The number on his career rank changed from 0 to 1. The System registered it with a single line:

```
[ CAREER RANK: UPDATED — COORDINATOR ]
[ NEW DIRECTIVE CATEGORY: ACTIVE ]
[ CONTINUE. ]
```

That is the close. He continues.

### Arc 2 Updated (T-021 to T-040)

Arc 2's original metaphor — the world opening — is now literal. The neighbourhood expanded. The workplace appeared. New people arrived who matter.

The Sync-Link partner's own career arc runs parallel. Her class (Architect) and his (Warlord) create natural complementarity. She solves problems he cannot see. He coordinates resources she cannot access. They are better at separate things in ways that neither of them entirely appreciates at first.

Her career arc is her own. It does not exist to serve his. Arc 2 gives her enough story to establish that.

### Arc 3 Updated (T-041 to T-060)

The Narrative Entity's attack in the original arc targets the MC's relationship with SYD. In the updated arc, it also targets his relationship with career progression.

The seeded doubt has a career face: his rank is performance, not reality. The Encounters he passed were too easy. The colleagues who seem to respect him are going through motions. He is one bad week away from losing it all.

This is the Narrative Entity's most sophisticated attack in the career dimension because it is unfalsifiable in the short term. The MC cannot prove the doubt wrong through argument. He can only prove it wrong by continuing to execute. The return to execution — not because the doubt disappeared, but despite it — is the arc's resolution.

## 6. Distribution — Instagram

**Existing chapter carousels:** Continue. The Life Track arc chapters distribute as before.

**New carousel category — Career Arc (T-015 onwards):**
When career arc chapters begin, a new visual format introduces the workplace context. The career Encounters visualise as workplace scenarios in the carousel. The slide sequence: scenario context → the MC's call → the outcome → the Skill Scroll that dropped. Final slide: the CTA.

**New content category — Encounter carousels:**
Short-form carousels that show a single encounter scenario (not from a chapter — standalone). The scenario, two decision options, the correct call revealed on the final slide with the Skill Scroll explanation. Ends with the CTA. Distributable independently of chapter release schedule.

**Caption voice:**
SYD voice. Bracket headers in the caption. One paragraph. Plants the chapter's hook. The link to /loop in the first comment or the bio.

## 7. Distribution — LinkedIn (/loop Page)

The /loop LinkedIn company page. Separate from any personal presence.

**Content strategy:**

**Career arc chapter excerpts:** The workplace-relevant sections of /signal chapters. Professional framing — the game context present but not required. The insight front-and-centre.

**Skill Scroll posts:** Individual Scrolls from the career track, formatted as professional insights. The game item aesthetic in the visual, the plain-language insight as the copy. Stat tag visible. Caption explains the real-world application.

**Stat-career mapping posts:** One stat, one career application, one real example from the /signal world. Single image or two-slide carousel.

**Voice on LinkedIn:**
SYD-adjacent but slightly warmer. Bracket headers used sparingly. The core philosophy translates directly: execution compounds, the gap is closeable, the system reflects reality. Professional audience — they know the gap the game is describing.
